How people are unintentionally killing whales with sound

The Whales

Whales in the ocean have lived their lives developing sensitive hearing
to be able to hear their surroundings instead of needing to see.
The main reasons whales developed this ability is because of the distance
sound can travel in water and that light doesn't travel far in water.
Whales adapted to using echolocation and sounds to get their
surroundings long before people started using ships.

The Ships

Cargo ships are the leading method of commercial item transportation,
but they're also the loudest for the ocean creatures.
With the amount of goods needing to be transported increasing by the year,
shipping companies have been using more cargo ships
which have big propellers. The big propellers flood the ocean with sound
which whales and other sea creatures are harmfully affected by.

The Waves

The sound waves that whales are hearing are very loud. With how far
waves can travel in water the navy and scientists have been using waves
to map out the ocean. These waves are very loud so they can be recieved
to be used in a map. These loud waves damage the whales ability to hear
anything else. Sound waves are known as a type of transverse wave.
